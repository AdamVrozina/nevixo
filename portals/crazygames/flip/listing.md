## Short description (listing cards / SEO — keep it short)

## Full description (game page)
FLIP is a one-tap gravity arcade game with a single rule: tap to flip.

Your ball races forward through a narrowing tunnel of blocks. Tap, click,
or press Space to flip gravity and snap to the opposite wall — time it right
to weave through the gaps and grab the gold coins along the way. The longer
you survive, the faster it gets.

Bank your coins between runs and spend them on a set of minimalist skins,
or watch a quick ad to revive after a crash and push your best distance even
further. Easy to learn in three seconds, hard to put down.

- One-button control — works on mobile and desktop
- Endless, procedurally generated runs that speed up as you go
- Collect coins and unlock cosmetic ball skins
- Revive after a crash and chase a new best distance
- Instant load, no downloads

## How to play (instructions field)
Your ball moves forward on its own. Tap, click, or press Space to flip
gravity and switch walls. Dodge the blocks, collect coins, and travel as far
as you can. Spend coins on skins, and watch an ad to revive if you crash.
