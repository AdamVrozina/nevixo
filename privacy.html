Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Nevixo ("we", "us") operates playnevixo.com and the games hosted on it. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

Data we collect

  • Analytics. We use Google Analytics (GA4) and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our games — pages viewed, session length, device type, approximate location, and in-game events (e.g. game started, game over). This data is aggregated and not used to personally identify you.
  • Advertising. We show ads via Google H5 Games Ads / AdSense, and on partner platforms via their own SDKs (e.g. CrazyGames). These providers may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted.
  • Local storage. Games save your progress and settings locally in your browser (localStorage). This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Contact form / newsletter. If you submit your email through our site (e.g. "Get updates" or our feedback form), we store it to send you occasional updates or to respond to feedback.

Cookies

We and our advertising and analytics partners use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and device identifiers) for analytics and to serve and measure ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third parties

When you play a game through a portal (such as CrazyGames), that platform's own privacy policy also applies to your visit there.

Our third-party partners include:

  • Google (Analytics, AdSense, H5 Games Ads) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Microsoft Clarity — privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

Children's privacy

Our games are intended for a general audience and are not directed specifically at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Where required, ads may be served in a non-personalized, contextual mode instead.

Your choices

You can disable cookies in your browser, use ad-blocking or tracking-protection tools, and opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the feedback link in the site footer.
